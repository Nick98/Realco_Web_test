Our History

Realco Transportation Group was founded in 1984 and has been engaging in providing services of port agency, liner agency and ship's chartering and brokering to customer's demand in the area of Taiwan, China and Vietnam. Raymond Tu, the Managing Director founded this company after his long management experience in Sea-Land service Inc. Maersk Lines and others, was once elected by the member companies of IOFFLAT (Sea Freight Forwarders Association) as the Chairman for two terms and the Director of TAFA (Air Freight Forwarders Association) in Taiwan in 1995. And the honorary positions of his leadership in these two associations are still remaining. Over in past two decades we have built our in-depth knowledge on the local market and operation as well as earned a great friendship and solid relationship with international ship owners and operators, cargo clients, governmental units, customs authorities, port authorities and terminal operators. What our belief is that customer's satisfaction is not only a business of ours but also a pleasure of ours, as we always deem service job a very personal business to deal with. Corporate of Realco Transportation Group is a compact but efficient organization with its head office situated in Taipei and branch offices in Taiwan - Taipei, Taoyuan Airport, Keelung, Taichung and Kaohsiung China - Shanghai, Ningbo, Hangzhou, Beijing, Qingdao, Nanjing, Xiamen, Shenzhen, Guangzhou, and Fuzhou. Hongkong Korea - Seoul Vietnam - Ho Chi Minh City, Hanoi and Haiphong And it consists of three core business in Air, Sea and Logistics Shipping agency - container liners and chemical tankers Ship chartering and brokering - chemical/gas tankers and dry bulk. The company has been ISO 9001:2015 accredited by Lloyd's Register since year of 1994 and 2004 respectively, and IATA/FIATA members are for over 25 years.

Why Choose Realco. Consulting?

Mr. Raymond Tu, Managing Director, founded the Realco Intercontinental Ltd. in 1984. In his previous career he gained management experience with Sea-Land Service Inc., Maersk Lines and others. He had been elected Chairman for two consecutive terms of IOFFLAT (Sea Freight Forwarders Association) and the Director of TAFA (Air Freight Forwarders Association) in Taiwan since 1995, and he is still entitled the honorary advisor in both associations now. “Over the past two decades we have developed an in-depth expertise of the local market allowing us to gain a good reputation and establish a solid link with international freight forwarders, logistics providers, shipping liners, airlines, shippers and consignees, ship owners and operators, cargo clients, governmental units, customs and port authorities, and terminal operators. We believe that customer's satisfaction is not only our business but also our pleasure. Working in a service business we are eager to show you our wholehearted dedication.” We have started our serving operation set-up in China (18) and Vietnam (3) operation besides of Taiwan (4) since 1990 simultaneously to keep on the pace of economic growth to serve our clients and working partners as a key player for a total solution in freight and logistics business over the East Asia area.
